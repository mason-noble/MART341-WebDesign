Frequently Asked Questions

What is Gourmet Coffee Club?

Gourmet Coffee Club is a subscription service that delivers freshly roasted premium coffee beans to your doorstep each month.

How does the subscription work?

Simply choose a subscription plan that suits your needs, and we'll deliver freshly roasted coffee beans to your door every month. You can manage your subscription online at any time.

Can I customize my order?

Yes! You can choose your preferred roast level and flavor profile. We also offer limited edition roasts that you can add to your order.

Is there a commitment?

No, you can cancel your subscription at any time. There are no long-term commitments or hidden fees.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we only ship within the United States. We are working on expanding our shipping options in the future.

How do I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team by emailing support@gourmetcoffeeclub.com or calling (555) 123-4567.
